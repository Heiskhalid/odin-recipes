Chicken Sauce Recipe


Description

This chicken apple sausage recipe with cabbage
makes a great cold weather meal, although
it's delicious anytime of the year.

Prep: 10 mins Cook: 45 mins Total: 55 mins Servings: 4

Ingredients

When making this dish, the following Ingredients are essential.

  • 1 ½ teaspoons butter
  • 1 teaspoon olive oil
  • 4 links chicken and apple sausage
  • 1 medium onion, sliced
  • salt and freshly ground black pepper to taste
  • 3 cloves garlic, crushed
  • 3 cups apple cider
  • 1 pound small Yukon Gold potatoes, scrubbed but not peeled
  • 1 ½ pounds finely shredded green cabbage
  • 2 tablespoons Dijon mustard, for garnish
  • 2 tablespoons chopped fresh parsley, for garnish

Directions

  1. Step 1
    Combine butter and olive oil in a Dutch oven over medium heat.
    When butter melts, add sausage links; cook until browned on all sides,
    6 to 8 minutes.
